# E-commerce-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline
🛒 E-comInsights: Global Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

A dynamic, interactive data visualization tool built to explore e-commerce transaction data worldwide—focusing on regional revenue trends, product category profitability, and customer purchasing behavior.

2. Short Description / Purpose
The E-comInsights Dashboard is a visually engaging and analytical Power BI report designed to help users explore sales performance across multiple countries and product lines. The dashboard focuses on highlighting major sales features, such as seasonal revenue peaks, regional growth patterns, and product demand. This tool is intended for use by retail analysts, marketing managers, and data-driven strategists who seek to understand trends and characteristics of e-commerce sales globally.

3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.

📂 Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the raw transaction data.

🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures (like YoY Growth and Profit Margin), dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.

📑 Data Modeling – Relationships established among tables (Sales, Products, Customers, and Geography) to enable cross-filtering and deep-dive analysis.

📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

4. Data Source
More info on where the data comes from and how it's structured:

Source: Global Retail Transaction Database & Marketing CRM exports.

Data on ~5,000+ transactions around the world, including details on their location, pricing, categories, and monthly sales performance for the fiscal year 2024–2025.

5. Features / Highlights
The best dashboard explanation format:

Business Problem
The global e-commerce industry generates billions in revenue, yet businesses often struggle to identify which specific regions or product types are underperforming in real-time.

Key questions addressed:

Which regions offer the most consistent month-over-month revenue growth?

Where is the highest concentration of "High Value" customers located?

What product categories have the highest return vs. purchase ratio?

Walkthrough of Key Visuals
Revenue Trend (Line Chart): Compares current sales against historical targets to understand seasonal trajectories.

Sales by Category (Stacked Bar Chart): Shows the breakdown of electronics, apparel, and home goods to help users gauge inventory levels and category capacity.

Regional Heatmap: Identifies geographic "hot spots" to guide logistics, shipping strategies, and localized marketing.

Business Impact & Insights
Marketing Optimization: Marketing teams can use this dashboard to identify key regions for targeted ad spend.

Strategic Expansion: Management can spot underdeveloped regions with high potential for new distribution hubs.

Operational Planning: Supply chain leads can plan inventory based on seasonal availability and demand peaks.
